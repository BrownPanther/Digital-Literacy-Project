## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

* Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required to send money
* Do not accept payment requests from unknown individuals
* Always verify the identity of the sender before making transactions
* Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or clicking suspicious links
* Enable app lock and two-factor authentication on banking apps
* Regularly check bank statements for unauthorized transactions
* Do not share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone
* Use only official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm
* Be cautious of calls pretending to be from banks or customer support

### 🚨 If Fraud Happens:

* Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call helpline: **1930 (24x7)**
